## Tips for Maximum Enhancement

### ✅ DO This:
- **Be specific**: "Python function using recursion" (not just "write a function")
- **List requirements**: Use separate lines or bullets
- **Mention format**: "return as JSON", "use bullet points"
- **Add context**: "for a e-commerce site", "beginner level"

### ❌ DON'T Do This:
- **Too vague**: "help me code"
- **No details**: Just a single word
- **Unclear goals**: "make something cool"
- **No format**: Don't specify output structure

## How the AI Reads Enhanced Prompts

When you paste an enhanced prompt into Claude or ChatGPT:

✅ **It understands the structure** - Clear sections make parsing easier
✅ **It knows priorities** - Requirements are clearly listed
✅ **It gets context** - Background is separated from task
✅ **It follows format** - Output expectations are explicit

This results in:
- ⚡ **Faster responses** - Less clarification needed
- 🎯 **Better quality** - AI understands exactly what you want
- 📦 **Proper structure** - Output matches your expectations
- 🔄 **Easy iteration** - Clear starting point for refinements

## Extension History Feature

### How It Works:
1. Every time you enhance a prompt, it's saved
2. Last 10 prompts are stored locally
3. Click any history item to view it again
4. Clear History button removes all saved items

### Privacy:
- History is stored **only on your computer**
- Not synced anywhere
- Not visible to anyone else
- Can be cleared anytime
